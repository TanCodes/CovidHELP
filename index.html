CvidHELP

CovidHELP website has been created to help people who need Oxygen, Beds, Plasma, Ventilators, etc.
This website contains the contact and location details of the resources available at that particular location.

Guide to use -
If anyone needs help just follow the steps:
1. Click on the "Search location", then select a particular or nearby location.
2. Then click on the required resource.
3. Click on view the details, contact them, and enquire about the availability.
